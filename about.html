About Us

Seattle Irish is a lightweight community hub for people connected to Ireland—Irish, Irish-American, recent arrivals, and friends of the culture—living in the Seattle area.

Mission

Make it easier to find the best places to gather—starting with pubs—and build from there: events, community groups, and local resources.
